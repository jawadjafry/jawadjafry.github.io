Award-winning media producer, documentary filmmaker, and video editor with two decades of senior leadership across television, documentary film, audio drama, podcast, educational media, and corporate communications.

Experienced live broadcast host and on-air presenter. Work screened before a Canadian Parliamentary subcommittee, licensed by universities in Canada, commissioned by the National Film Board of Canada, and broadcast nationally on OMNI TV.

Co-founder of Nayla Media, an independent educational media company built from zero to 87.7M views and 185K YouTube subscribers through original content strategy, end-to-end production, and platform-native distribution.

Fluent in English, Urdu, and Hindi, with working Punjabi and familiarity with Classical Arabic. Extensive experience producing multilingual content for South Asian and Muslim community audiences across North America.
